About Me

My name is David Effiong and I transitioned from Professional Banking to Data Analytics. Becoming a data analyst from scratch took me 2 years of active study alongside my busy 7am to 7pm banking operations job. It took alot of mistakes and mentoring to figure out what was really important to learn in my journey. My goal here is to shorten the time it takes you especially as a busy professional to learn the fundamentals of data analysis, and get your hands busy with basic analytics tools.

Learning Paths

  1. What is Data Analysis all about?
  2. Excel as tool for Analysis
  3. Using SQL for Analysis
  4. Using Data Studio or Power BI for visualization
  5. Hands on Projects

Expectations

At the end of the four month virtual training, you would have developed strong fundamentals of data analysis, completed some projects and fit to enter intermediate level learning of Data Analysis. The program requires 3 hours per week with tutor virtually and additional 4 hours for your personal practice and exercises. The lesson formats includes video calls, assignments and projects.
